Privacy Policy

Welcome to Asha Computer. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use our services, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard that information.

1. Information Gathering and Usage

When you visit Asha Computer, we may collect information that identifies you personally only when you voluntarily submit it. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, and academic interests when you interact with our forms or registration portals. We use this data to:

  • Personalize your educational experience on our platform.
  • Respond to your technical queries and provide support.
  • Send periodic updates regarding new tutorials or site features.

2. Data Protection & Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We use advanced encryption protocols to protect sensitive data transmitted online. Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who are required to keep the information confidential.

3. Log Data and Analytics

Like most website operators, Asha Computer collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available. This includes the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Our purpose in collecting this is to better understand how our visitors use the website so we can optimize our performance and content.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer. We use cookies to help us identify and track visitors, their usage of our website, and their website access preferences. Visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using our website, though certain features may not function properly without them.

5. Third-Party Links

Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third-party products or services on our website. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We, therefore, have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

Trust and Transparency

"At Asha Computer, transparency is the foundation of our relationship with our students. We believe you have the right to know how your data is handled, and we promise to treat your information with the highest level of integrity."
